Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for programs?

Fill out our contact form above and select the program you're interested in. We'll get back to you with application details and requirements.

Are the programs free?

Yes! All our programs are completely free thanks to our sponsors and donors. We believe technology education should be accessible to everyone.

What are the requirements?

Requirements vary by program. Generally, you need to be motivated to learn and commit to attending sessions. No prior experience required for beginner programs.

How can I volunteer?

We welcome volunteers! Contact us using the form above and select "Volunteer Opportunities" to learn about current needs and how you can contribute.

Can I join the Wikipedia project?

Absolutely! Visit our Wikimedia Community Kilimanjaro page or contact us to get started.

Do you offer job placement?

Yes, we provide career support and job placement assistance for our program graduates, including resume help and interview preparation.
