About Youth Tech

Empowering the next generation of Tanzanian tech leaders

Our Mission

Youth Tech is a non-governmental organization dedicated to empowering Tanzanian youth through technology education and digital innovation. We believe that access to technology and digital skills is essential for creating opportunities and driving economic growth in Tanzania.

Founded with the vision of bridging the digital divide, we provide comprehensive technology education programs, mentorship opportunities, and practical experience through real-world projects like our Wikipedia initiative.

Our Vision

To create a Tanzania where every young person has access to technology education and the skills needed to thrive in the digital economy.

Our Values

  • Inclusivity: Technology education for all
  • Innovation: Creative problem-solving
  • Community: Collaborative learning environment
  • Excellence: High-quality education standards

Our Wikipedia Project

As part of our commitment to digital literacy and knowledge sharing, Youth Tech actively participates in the Wikimedia Community Kilimanjaro project. Our volunteers contribute to Wikipedia by creating and editing articles about Tanzania, East Africa, and topics relevant to our community.

This initiative not only improves digital literacy skills but also ensures that Tanzanian perspectives and knowledge are represented in the world's largest encyclopedia.
